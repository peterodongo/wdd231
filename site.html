Site Name: ELEARN

site purpose

ELEARN is your one-stop destination for online learning resources and courses that help improve the student's learning experience as well as their performance in school while giving updated information to parents

Color scheme:

The color scheme for ELEARN is designed to be visually appealing and easy on the eyes, with a primary color of grey, black and white.

The primary color of grey is used for the background and main elements of the site, creating a neutral and calming atmosphere. The black color is used for header and footer as well as with texts which will make it easy to read. The white color is used for accents and highlights, adding a clean and modern touch to the overall design.

Typography:

The typography for ELEARN is designed to be clear and easy to read, with a focus on legibility and accessibility. The primary font used on the site is "Roboto", a sans-serif font that is modern and clean. This font is used for all body text, headings, and other important elements on the site.

For headings and other important elements, a bold version of the "Roboto" font is used to create emphasis and hierarchy. This helps to guide the user's attention and make it easy to navigate the site.
